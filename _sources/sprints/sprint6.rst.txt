Sprint #6
=================================================

Goals
----------------

  1) Synchronise our artefacts to Standards 0.9.3 (particularly Product Reference Data)
  2) Focus on Product Reference Data deliverables    
  3) Prepare and Execute Engineering Workshop #2 scheduled for 13 June 2019

Deliverables
-------------------

    2) Conformance reporting for PRD
