Desktop Sandbox (desktop-sandbox)¶

Summary

The Desktop Sandbox is intended to be a self contained but complete representation of the emerging Consumer Data Standards ecosystem. It achieves this goal through the programmatic coordination of multiple subservices combining the various other CDS Engineering artefacts.
